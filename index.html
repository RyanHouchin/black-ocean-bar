Method

  1. Brown the butter over medium heat until golden and nutty. Pull off heat. Stir in ghee, cashew butter, and honey until smooth.
  2. Let cool to warm. Dissolve shilajit in the fat. Stir in royal jelly, egg yolks, and vanilla.
  3. Whisk all dry ingredients together with the organ capsule powder in a separate bowl.
  4. Pour wet into dry. Mix until a thick, cohesive dough forms.
  5. Fold in cacao nibs and gelatin powder.
  6. Scoop with a cookie scoop. Roll into balls.
  7. Roll each ball in strawberry powder.
  8. Refrigerate one hour. Done.
